Wrapped BTC Assets (WBTC, cbBTC, tBTC)

Overview

Various wrapped versions of Bitcoin for DeFi use.

WBTC (Wrapped Bitcoin)

Basics

  • Custodian: BitGo (primary)
  • Market Cap: ~$10B+
  • Chains: Ethereum, others
  • Mechanism: Centralized custody

Risk Profile

  • BitGo custody (regulated US company)
  • 1:1 BTC backing, audited
  • Centralization risk
  • Can blacklist addresses
  • Score: 4/5

cbBTC (Coinbase Wrapped Bitcoin)

Basics

  • Custodian: Coinbase
  • Market Cap: Growing
  • Chains: Ethereum, Base
  • Mechanism: Coinbase custody

Risk Profile

  • Coinbase custody (NASDAQ listed)
  • Highly regulated
  • 1:1 backing
  • Newer than WBTC
  • Score: 4/5

Wrapped BTC Pairs (WBTC/cbBTC)

Why Low IL

  • Both track BTC price
  • Minor deviations only
  • Essentially BTC-BTC pair
  • IL risk: negligible

Yield Sources

  • Trading fees from arbitrage
  • LP incentives
  • Very safe for BTC exposure

Summary

Asset Custodian Score Notes
WBTC BitGo 4/5 Established, centralized
cbBTC Coinbase 4/5 Regulated, newer
tBTC Threshold 3.5/5 Decentralized, smaller

Wrapped BTC pairs are excellent for low-IL yield strategies.
