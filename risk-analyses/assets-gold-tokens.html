Gold Tokens (PAXG, XAUT)

Overview

Tokenized gold for DeFi use.

PAXG (Pax Gold)

Basics

  • Issuer: Paxos Trust Company
  • Backing: 1:1 physical gold
  • Custody: Brink's vaults (London)
  • Regulation: NYDFS regulated

Risk Profile

  • 1 PAXG = 1 troy oz gold
  • Allocated, serialized bars
  • Monthly attestations
  • US regulated
  • Score: 4.5/5

XAUT (Tether Gold)

Basics

  • Issuer: Tether
  • Backing: 1:1 physical gold
  • Custody: Switzerland
  • Regulation: Less clear

Risk Profile

  • Same Tether transparency concerns
  • Physical gold backing
  • Swiss custody
  • Score: 3.5/5

Gold Pairs (PAXG/XAUT)

Why Low IL

  • Both track gold price
  • Minor deviations only
  • Essentially gold-gold pair
  • IL risk: negligible

Yield Sources

  • Trading fees from arbitrage
  • Lower volume than crypto pairs
  • Yields typically 4-8% APR

Risk Scores

Asset Issuer Score Notes
PAXG Paxos 4.5/5 NYDFS regulated, transparent
XAUT Tether 3.5/5 Tether concerns apply

Summary

Gold tokens provide exposure to gold prices on-chain. PAXG is more transparent and regulated. Pairs are excellent for low-IL yield with gold exposure.
