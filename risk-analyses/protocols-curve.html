Incidents

  • July 2023: Vyper compiler vulnerability affected some pools (~$70M drained across ecosystem). Not a Curve contract bug but compiler issue. Curve responded quickly with patches.
  • Funds partially recovered. Protocol has continued operating safely since.

Summary

Curve is a cornerstone of DeFi infrastructure with exceptional security practices. Multiple audits from top firms (Trail of Bits, ChainSecurity, Quantstamp), active bug bounty, and years of battle-testing. The 2023 Vyper incident was a compiler bug, not a Curve design flaw, and was handled professionally. One of the safest protocols in DeFi for stablecoin trading and yield.
