Summary

SparkLend combines Aave V3's battle-tested lending infrastructure with MakerDAO's DAI ecosystem. Strong security inheritance from Aave, plus MakerDAO's governance and backing. The D3M ensures deep DAI liquidity. One of the safest lending protocols for DAI/USDS yield strategies.
