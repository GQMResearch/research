Economic Model

Yield Source

  • Trading fees (variable by pool type)
  • AERO emissions (via gauge voting)
  • Bribes from protocols seeking liquidity

Pool Types

  1. Stable Pools: For pegged assets (low fees)
  2. Volatile Pools: For non-pegged pairs
  3. Concentrated Liquidity (Slipstream): Higher efficiency, higher IL risk

Token (AERO)

  • Governance and emissions
  • veAERO for voting power and fee share
  • Emissions schedule reduces over time

Sustainability

  • Strong fee generation from Base activity
  • Bribes create additional demand for veAERO
  • Ecosystem incentives from Base/Coinbase

Concentrated Liquidity (CL) Specific Risks

For CL pools on Aerodrome:

  1. Active Management Required: Positions can go out of range
  2. Higher IL Risk: Concentrated positions amplify impermanent loss
  3. Complexity: More sophisticated than standard AMM
  4. Range Selection: Wrong range = no fees earned

Summary

Aerodrome is the dominant DEX on Base with strong security inherited from Velodrome. ve(3,3) tokenomics create sustainable incentives. CL pools offer high APRs but require understanding of IL and range management. Best for stablecoin pairs and correlated assets where IL is minimal.
