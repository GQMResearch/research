sdToken Risks

What are sdTokens?

  • Liquid staking derivatives of locked tokens
  • e.g., sdCRV = liquid version of veCRV
  • Allows trading while earning voting rewards

Risks

  1. Depeg Risk: sdToken can trade below 1:1 with underlying
  2. Smart Contract Layer: Additional contracts on top of base protocol
  3. Liquidity: May have thin liquidity for large exits
  4. Dependency: Relies on underlying protocol (Curve, etc.)

Summary

Stake DAO is a solid yield aggregator with a focus on liquid lockers for governance tokens. Less audited than blue-chip protocols but has maintained a clean record. Good for accessing boosted Curve yields without locking. Medium risk - suitable for moderate allocations rather than core positions.
