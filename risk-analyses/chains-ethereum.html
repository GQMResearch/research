Ethereum

Overview

  • Type: Layer 1 (PoS)
  • Consensus: Proof of Stake (since September 2022)
  • Launched: July 2015
  • Website: https://ethereum.org
  • Status: Most battle-tested smart contract platform

Security Model

Consensus

  • Proof of Stake with ~1M+ validators
  • 32 ETH minimum stake
  • Slashing for misbehavior
  • Finality: ~12-15 minutes

Decentralization

  • Most decentralized smart contract platform
  • No single point of failure
  • Global validator distribution
  • Client diversity improving

Track Record

  • 8+ years operational
  • No major chain-level exploits
  • Survived multiple market crashes
  • Regular upgrades without issues

For DeFi Users

Strengths

  • Maximum security for large positions
  • Deepest liquidity
  • Most audited protocols
  • Native asset security

Considerations

  • Higher gas fees
  • Slower finality than L2s
  • May be overkill for small positions

Risk Scores

Summary

Ethereum is the gold standard for blockchain security. 8+ years of track record, highly decentralized, and regularly stress-tested. Best for large positions where security is paramount.
