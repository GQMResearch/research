How It Works

  1. Protocols deposit incentive tokens
  2. Merkl calculates rewards based on on-chain activity
  3. Users claim via Merkle proofs

Merkl-Specific Risks

What to Watch

  1. Incentive Duration: Check how long rewards last
  2. Depositing Protocol: Who's paying the rewards?
  3. APY Sustainability: High APYs often temporary campaigns
  4. Off-chain Trust: Reward calculation is off-chain

USP HOLD Example

  • Very high APY (59%+) suggests heavy short-term incentives
  • Verify USP protocol's backing
  • Check campaign duration
  • Likely not sustainable long-term

Summary

Merkl is a reputable incentive distribution platform backed by the well-known Angle team. Smart contracts are relatively simple (claim mechanism) and audited. Main risk is that rewards depend on external protocols depositing incentives - always research the source of yield, not just the distribution mechanism.
