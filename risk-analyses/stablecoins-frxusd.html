FRXUSD (Frax USD)

Overview

  • Type: Hybrid stablecoin
  • Issuer: Frax Finance
  • Backing: AMO-managed assets
  • Market Cap: ~$500M+ (FRAX ecosystem)
  • Chains: Ethereum, Fraxtal
  • Website: https://frax.finance

Evolution Note

  • Original FRAX: Partially algorithmic
  • FRAX v3 / FRXUSD: Moving to 100% collateralized
  • Currently transitioning to full backing

Mechanism

AMO (Algorithmic Market Operations)

  • Protocol-controlled strategies
  • Deploys collateral for yield
  • Maintains peg actively
  • More capital efficient than static reserves

Collateral Strategy

  • USDC
  • sDAI (DAI Savings Rate)
  • sFRAX (Frax staking)
  • Treasury bonds (via partners)

Peg Maintenance

  • AMOs actively arbitrage
  • Curve pool management
  • Frax lending market

Risks

  1. Complexity: Multiple mechanisms
  2. Historical: Was partially algorithmic
  3. AMO Risk: Active strategies can have issues
  4. Dependency: Uses other stables (USDC)

Historical Performance

Summary

FRXUSD represents Frax's evolution toward a fully-backed stablecoin. More complex than pure fiat-backed stables due to AMO mechanisms, but managed by a capable team. Good for DeFi strategies, especially within Frax ecosystem. Moderate risk due to complexity and dependence on other stables.
