2. Project Overview

What It Does

Pendle Finance is a yield tokenization protocol that splits interest-bearing assets into two components:

  • PT (Principal Token) — Represents the principal, redeemable at maturity for the underlying
  • YT (Yield Token) — Represents the future yield stream until maturity

This enables fixed-rate yields (buy PT at discount), yield speculation (buy YT), and sophisticated yield strategies.

Key Innovation

  • First major protocol to bring fixed-income concepts to DeFi
  • Custom AMM designed for time-decaying assets
  • vePENDLE system for governance and revenue sharing

Roadmap

Delivered:

3. How It Works (User Perspective)

High-Level Explanation

  1. User deposits yield-bearing asset (e.g., stETH, sUSDe)
  2. Pendle mints PT + YT tokens with a maturity date
  3. User can sell YT for immediate cash (locking in fixed rate via PT)
  4. Or sell PT and keep YT to speculate on higher yields
  5. At maturity, PT redeems 1:1 for underlying

User Roles & Incentives

PT Buyers (Fixed Yield)

  • Actions: Buy PT at discount, hold to maturity
  • Incentives: Guaranteed fixed yield regardless of rate changes
  • Risks: Opportunity cost if rates rise significantly

YT Buyers (Yield Speculators)

  • Actions: Buy YT to bet on high yields
  • Incentives: Leveraged exposure to yield movements
  • Risks: YT can go to zero if yields disappoint

Liquidity Providers

  • Actions: Provide liquidity to PT/underlying pools
  • Incentives: Trading fees + PENDLE incentives
  • Risks: Impermanent loss, smart contract risk

vePENDLE Holders

  • Actions: Lock PENDLE for vePENDLE
  • Incentives: Boosted yields, governance, protocol revenue share
  • Risks: Lock-up period, token price risk
